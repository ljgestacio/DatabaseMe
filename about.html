Our founding

It began as a challenge in my workplace, when my manager asks me to create a recording system for the employee’s training record to track their performance during training. Back then the filing application used is Microsoft Office Excel which is difficult to navigate through several worksheets.

Growth & beyond

Currently, we have successfully created a queuing system, transactions tracking and reporting databases, several employee’s database, online work request system, invoices and billings management system, facilities reservations system, to name a few of our projects.
